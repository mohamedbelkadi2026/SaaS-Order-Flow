FIX — Driver phone (numéro livreur) only shows on "Suivi des colis". Make it show on every carrier-related page.
Problem
In client/src/pages/orders.tsx, the driver phone is conditional on urlStatus === 'suivi_group'. So it appears on the Suivi des Colis page but NOT on:

En cours (urlStatus === 'in_progress')
Livrées (urlStatus === 'delivered')
Refusées (urlStatus === 'refused')

Once an order has a tracking number and a driver assigned, the driver phone is useful in every downstream view — call the driver to chase a delivery, ask why it bounced, etc.
